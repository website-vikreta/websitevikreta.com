# Target Users — Website Vikreta

## Primary: SMB Owner / Founder (10–200 employees)
**Psychographic:**
- Overwhelmed. Wearing too many hats.
- Knows AI is coming but doesn't know where to start.
- Has tried some tools. Got confused. Gave up.
- Trusts execution over theory.
- Judges agencies by: "Do they get MY industry?"

**Pain they feel:**
- "My team is drowning in repetitive work"
- "My competitors are moving faster"
- "I've wasted money on agencies that overpromised"
- "I don't have a tech team to implement this"

**What makes them hire us:**
- We understand their problem before we pitch a solution
- We speak outcomes, not tools
- We show proof (case studies, results, numbers)
- They feel relieved, not sold to

**Hero emotion target:** RELIEVED. "Finally, someone who gets it."

---

## Secondary: Mid-Market Marketing / Ops Director (200–1000 employees)
**Psychographic:**
- Has budget. Needs accountability.
- Reports to a C-suite. Needs ROI narrative.
- Has an internal team but lacks AI expertise.
- Risk-averse. Needs credibility signals.

**Pain they feel:**
- "Our processes don't scale with our growth"
- "We're paying too many vendors for disconnected tools"
- "I need to show impact to leadership"

**What makes them hire us:**
- Case studies with numbers
- Clear methodology (not black box)
- Senior point of contact, not junior hand-off

---

## What Both Users Share
- Time-poor. Dense content must be scannable.
- Mobile-first behavior (research on phone, decision on desktop)
- Trust content before trusting a company
- Influenced by: How the website feels, not just what it says
